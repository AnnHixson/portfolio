About Me

This is the "About Me" page of my portfolio (in progress)

My name is Ann Hixson. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Cognitive Science from the University of Connecticut (UCONN). In my studies, I particularly enjoyed and excelled at both symbolic logic (Philosophy) and syntax (Linguistics). This love of problem solving and logical thinking prompted me to look into computer programming. To that end, I recently completed a six month long intensive Coding Boot Camp with the UCONN School of Engineering.
